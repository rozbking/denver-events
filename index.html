🌙 Slow Nights → Themed Movie Night or Chaos Bloom

When there's nothing calling: Roll for a themed movie night from the ideas below, or check Chaos Bloom's calendar. These nights are perfect when you're tired, need to save money, or just want cozy in-house vibes.
🎬 Themed Movie Night Ideas
Heist Night: Ocean's trilogy → Italian Job → Fancy cocktails, dress up
Comfort Classics: Princess Bride → When Harry Met Sally → Cozy blankets, popcorn bar
Sci-Fi Double: Blade Runner 2049 → Ex Machina → LED lights, futuristic snacks
Studio Ghibli Marathon: Pick 2-3 → Homemade ramen, Japanese snacks
Musical Madness: La La Land → Moulin Rouge → Sing along, dress glamorous
True Crime Double: Zodiac → Parasite → Themed snacks, true crime podcast after
Cozy Horror: Knives Out → Clue → Murder mystery cocktails
Retro SciFi: 2001: A Space Odyssey → Arrival → Mind-bending snacks
Chaos Bloom: Check their calendar for weird, wonderful experimental events
